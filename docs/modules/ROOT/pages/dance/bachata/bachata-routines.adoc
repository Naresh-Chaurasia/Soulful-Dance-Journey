== Practice Routines

=== Level 0

* Side Basic x 2
* Front and Back Basic x 2
* Left Turn x 2
* Right Turn x 2

=== Level 1

* Basic + 180 degree (rotation)
* Bachata Turns for Leader and Follower
* Wrap Or Pretzel 
* Hair Comb Or Caricia | Leader and Follower
* Hammer Lock | Hammer Lock with Cross Hands
* Mambo Or Step Tap, Step Tap

=== Level 2

* Switching Places
* Neck Roll
* Wave
* Kentucky | Pretzel | Wrap (Take in Opp Direction | Use Dance Floor)
