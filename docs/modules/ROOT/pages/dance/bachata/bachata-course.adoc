##############################################

This curriculum is designed to help complete beginners develop a strong foundation in Bachata by balancing:

* Solo footwork
* Body movement
* Partner connection
* Social dancing

The focus is *quality over quantity*. Rather than teaching many patterns, students should become comfortable with timing, leading, following, musicality, and confidence on the dance floor.

== Level 1 Outcome

> By the end of this course, students should be able to:

* Dance confidently to Bachata music.
* Lead or follow comfortably using proper connection.
* Perform beginner turns and social dance combinations.
* Understand musical timing and rhythm.
* Transition smoothly between common dance positions.
* Dance socially with different partners while maintaining good etiquette.

[NOTE]
====
Focus on *quality over quantity* — rather than teaching many patterns, students should become comfortable with timing, leading, following, musicality, and confidence on the dance floor.
====
