== Bachata Basics Drills

[cols="1,3,2", options="header"]
|===
| Day | Practice Drill (15–30 mins) | What They Are Learning

| Day 1
a|
*Warm-up*

* In Place Basic (1 min)
* Side Basic (1 min)
* Front & Back Basic (1 min)

*Solo Fundamentals*

* Side Basic (1 min)
* Front & Back Basic (1 min)
* In Place Basic (1 min)
* Step and Tap (1 min)

*Partner Work*

* Side Basic in Open Hold (2 min)
* Front & Back Basic in Open Hold (2 min)
a|
* Timing awareness
* Weight transfer
* Basic muscle memory

| Day 2
a|
*Warm-up*

* In Place Basic (1 min)
* Side Basic (1 min)

*Solo Turn Practice*

* Left Turn (2 min)
* Right Turn (2 min)

*Partner Work*

* Side Basic → Left Turn
* Side Basic → Right Turn
* Repeat for 5–7 mins
a|
* Spin balance
* Body coordination
* Turn mechanics

| Day 3
a|
*Direction Drill*

* Horizontal Basic (2 min)
* Vertical Basic (2 min)
* Diagonal Basic (2 min)
* Circular Basic (2 min)

*Partner Work*

* Front–Back Basic
* Side Basic
* Repeat continuously
a|
* Spatial awareness
* Foot placement control
* Movement in different directions

| Day 4
a|
*Warm-up Basics*

*Transition Drill*

* Open Hold → Semi Close Hold
* Side Basic
* Front & Back Basic
* In Place Basic

* Change hold every 8 counts
a|
* Partner connection
* Frame control
* Smooth transitions

| Day 5
a|
*Turn Combination Drill*

Sequence:

* Side Basic
* Right Turn
* Front & Back Basic
* Left Turn
* Step and Tap

* Repeat continuously for 10–12 mins
a|
* Combining moves
* Lead and follow timing
* Dance flow

| Day 6
a|
*Continuous Dancing*

Repeat:

* Side Basic
* Front & Back Basic
* Left Turn
* Right Turn
* Step and Tap
* Direction Basics

* Dance continuously for 15 mins
a|
* Muscle memory reinforcement
* Stamina
* Confidence while dancing

| Day 7
a|
*Social Dance Practice*

* Play 3–4 salsa songs
* Dance freely
* Use both holds
* Use turns
* Use basics in different directions
a|
* Real dance application
* Improvisation
* Comfort with partner
|===
